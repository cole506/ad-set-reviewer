📋 Set Requirements
  • 15 total creatives per set
  • 5× Singles — Trust or Service only
  • 5× Two-Folds — paired different types
  • 5× Tri-Folds — Trust + Service + Brand
  • Extra videos allowed (optional)
  • Never reuse a creative across sets
🚫 Auto-Deny Triggers
  • Blurred or jumbled logo
  • Unreadable AI-generated text
  • Duplicate faces in one image
  • Subject cut off at edges
  • Severe over/under exposure
  • Brand-only Single (no Trust or Service present)
  • Generic AI faces when real reps exist
📸 Photo Types
  • Trust — happy person(s), positive feeling
  • Service — roofing/HVAC work, completed or WIP
  • Brand — vehicle, branded clothes, lawn sign
